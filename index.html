Our Story 🧶

Where every stitch is a memory, and every creation is made with love

Hi, I'm the heart behind By Spookiee — a young creator who fell in love with crochet and never looked back. What started as a quiet hobby, loop by loop, slowly became something much more meaningful: a way to turn simple yarn into gifts that make people genuinely smile.

Whether it's a bridal bouquet that will stay beautiful forever, a tiny keychain gifted to a best friend, or a mystery box filled with cozy surprises — each piece is made with patience, love, and a little bit of magic.
